Fencing in Sorrento

We provide expert fence repairs, restorations, re-fencing and fence painting across Sorrento and surrounding suburbs. Our materials and coatings are chosen for Peninsula coastal conditions and installed to Australian Standards.

Sorrento's stunning clifftop location and historic charm make it one of the Peninsula's most sought-after addresses, but the extreme coastal conditions demand specialized fencing expertise. From the limestone heritage buildings in the village to modern architect-designed homes with ocean views, we understand the unique challenges of working in this exposed coastal environment where salt air, strong winds, and heritage considerations intersect.

Our team is experienced with Sorrento's specific requirements, from heritage overlay restrictions to the demanding weather conditions that test even the highest quality fencing materials. We've worked extensively throughout the area including Ocean Beach Road, the Point Nepean Road corridor, and the exclusive residential streets near the golf course. Whether maintaining a historic cottage or servicing a contemporary home, we provide solutions designed to withstand Sorrento's beautiful but challenging coastal environment.

  • Leak detection & fence repairs
  • Full fence restorations & coatings
  • Tile or metal re-fencing
  • Professional fence painting
