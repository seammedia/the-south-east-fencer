Frequently Asked Questions

Do you build composite decks?
Yes—we install both timber and composite decking. We'll recommend the best option based on your budget, maintenance preferences and aesthetic goals.
Do I need engineering for retaining walls?
We'll advise based on wall height, soil type and local council rules. Walls over 1m typically require engineering certification.
Can you integrate stairs and screens?
Absolutely—we design and build stairs, privacy screens and planters as part of a cohesive outdoor solution.
How long does a deck or retaining wall project take?
Typical decks take 3–7 days; retaining walls vary with height and length. We'll give you a clear timeline in your quote.
