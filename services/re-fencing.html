Re-Fencing

When it's beyond repair, we replace with modern, durable materials for longevity, performance and energy efficiency — including proper insulation and ventilation.

Get a quote All services

When to re-fence

  • Widespread rusting, corrosion, or tile fatigue affecting multiple areas
  • Chronic leaks despite repeated patching and repairs
  • Major renovations where improved insulation, sarking, and a fresh look are desired
  • Structural changes requiring modified fence lines or additional load capacity
  • Energy efficiency upgrades to reduce heating and cooling costs
  • Storm damage that makes replacement more economical than extensive repairs

Material options

We specialize in Colorbond and Zincalume metal re-fencing, as well as tile-to-metal conversions. Modern metal fencing offers superior durability, weather resistance, and lower maintenance compared to traditional materials.

Our installations include proper fence blanket insulation, reflective sarking, and ventilation systems designed for Peninsula conditions. This combination delivers excellent thermal performance and condensation control — crucial for coastal homes.

Our process

We begin with careful removal and disposal of existing fencing materials, followed by thorough inspection of fence framing. Any structural repairs are completed before installing modern sarking, insulation, and the new fencing system.

Each installation includes proper flashings, fixings rated for local wind conditions, and detailed weatherpfencing around penetrations. We work systematically to maintain weather protection throughout the project.

Insulation and comfort

A re-fence is the perfect opportunity to upgrade insulation and ventilation. We install bulk insulation (typically R1.8 to R2.5), reflective sarking, and adequate ventilation to create a comfortable, energy-efficient home.

Proper insulation dramatically reduces heat transfer, keeping homes cooler in summer and warmer in winter while reducing rain noise — a common concern with metal fencing.

FAQs

Can we keep living in the home?

Yes — we stage works carefully and make the fence watertight each day. Most families can remain in their homes throughout the process with minimal disruption.

How long does it take?

Most residential re-fences complete in 2–5 working days depending on size, complexity, and weather conditions. We'll provide a detailed timeline in your quote.

What about council approval?

Most like-for-like replacements don't require permits, but we'll advise on any requirements specific to your property and handle necessary approvals where needed.

Do you handle the insurance process?

We regularly work with insurance assessors and can provide detailed reports and quotes for storm damage claims. We'll guide you through the process when applicable.

Quality assurance

All our re-fencing work comes with comprehensive warranties covering both materials and installation. We use only proven products from established manufacturers and follow strict installation standards.

Our team is experienced in Peninsula conditions and understands the specific requirements for coastal installations including appropriate fixing densities and corrosion-resistant hardware.

Service area: We cover the entire Peninsula including Mount Eliza, Mornington, Frankston, Sorrento and surrounding suburbs.
