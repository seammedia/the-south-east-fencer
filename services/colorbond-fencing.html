Colorbond Fencing FAQ

How long does Colorbond fencing last?
With proper installation, Colorbond fencing typically lasts 30-40+ years in coastal conditions. BlueScope's Activar¬Æ coating protects against corrosion and weathering.
What fence heights are available?
Standard residential heights are 1.5m, 1.8m, and 2.1m. We can also accommodate custom heights depending on your privacy and council requirements.
Do I need council approval?
Most standard residential fences under 2m don't require a permit, but boundary fences and heritage areas may. We can advise on local Mornington Peninsula council requirements.
How much does Colorbond fencing cost?
Pricing depends on fence height, length, ground conditions, and colour choice. Contact us for a free, no-obligation quote tailored to your property.
