How It Works

Site Check & Measure

We visit your property to measure boundaries, check ground conditions and discuss your style preferences.

Options & Quote

You'll receive a clear quote outlining materials, height, finish and timeframe—no hidden surprises.

Materials & Schedule

Once confirmed, we order your materials and lock in a start date that suits your calendar.

Install & Clean Up

Our crew installs posts, rails and panels with care, then removes all waste and leaves your site tidy.

Final Walk-Through

We walk you through the finished fence, answer any questions and ensure you're completely satisfied.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does installation take?
Most straight runs are 1–2 days; complex sites can take longer. We'll give you an accurate timeframe in your quote.
Which materials do you install?
Colorbond®, timber, feature screens, pool-compliant solutions and more. We'll recommend the best option for your needs and budget.
Can you remove the old fence?
Yes, we can remove and dispose of your old fence as part of the job—just let us know during quoting.
Do you handle council permits?
We'll advise on local requirements and can assist with council applications where needed.
