Home » Services » Fencing Repair

A damaged fence isn't just an eyesore—it can compromise security, privacy and property value. Whether it's storm damage, a vehicle impact, rotting timber or leaning posts, our repair service brings fences back to safe, solid condition with minimal disruption. We match existing styles where possible, use quality materials and complete most jobs in 1–3 days. No job too small.

Why Choose Our Fencing Repair Service

Fast Response

We understand urgent repairs can't wait—we prioritise safety issues and work to your timeline.

Cost-Effective Fixes

Repair what's damaged, not the whole fence—we'll save you money by targeting the problem areas.

Match Existing Style

We source close material and colour matches so repairs blend seamlessly with your current fence.

Clean Site on Completion

All waste removed, site swept clean—you'll barely know we were there apart from the fixed fence.

How It Works

Photo/Measure

Send us photos or we'll visit to assess the damage and measure what needs replacing.

Fixed-Price Quote

You'll get a clear quote outlining parts, labour and timeframe—no hidden costs.

Repair Works

Our crew replaces posts, rails, panels or fixings using quality materials and proven methods.

Finish & Tidy

We check alignment, secure all fasteners and remove all waste before calling the job done.

We'll source close matches in material and colour to blend seamlessly with your existing fence.
