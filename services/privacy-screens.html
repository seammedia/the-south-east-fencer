Whether you want to shield an outdoor dining area from neighbours, add architectural interest to a plain fence or create privacy without blocking light and airflow, custom privacy screens deliver style and function in equal measure. We design and install screens using timber slats, powder-coated aluminium, composite panels and decorative perforated designs—tailored to your home's aesthetic and council regulations. Perfect for balconies, decks, pool areas and boundary features.

Why Choose Our Privacy Screen Service

Custom Designs

We work with your home's style, existing fencing and landscaping to create screens that complement, not clash.

Light & Airflow Control

Choose solid panels for full privacy or slatted/perforated designs that let light and breezes through.

Durable Finishes

Treated timber, powder-coated metals and composite materials built to handle Australian sun, rain and coastal conditions.

Low Maintenance Options

Aluminium and composite screens require minimal upkeep—no painting, staining or sealing needed.

Frequently Asked Questions

What materials are best for privacy screens?
Timber, powder-coated aluminium, composite and custom decorative panels all work well. We'll recommend based on your aesthetic goals, maintenance preferences and budget.
Can screens be semi-transparent?
Yes—you can choose slat spacings or perforated designs that allow light and airflow while still providing privacy.
Are screens council compliant?
We'll guide you on local height and setback rules to ensure your privacy screen meets council requirements.
How long do privacy screens last?
Quality timber screens last 10–15+ years with proper treatment; aluminium and composite options can last 20–30+ years with minimal maintenance.
