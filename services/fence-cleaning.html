Why clean your fence?

  • Removes moss, mould, lichen and salt build-up
  • Restores colour and lifts street appeal
  • Helps protect coatings & materials from premature wear
  • Essential preparation for fence painting or restoration
  • Carried out by local, insured specialists who work to Australian Standards

What we clean

  • Concrete & terracotta tile fences
  • Colorbond® / metal fences
  • Ridge cappings, valleys & flashings
  • Edge cleaning around solar panels (panel glass not washed)
  • Optional: gutters, fascia & downpipe exteriors

We select the safest effective method for your fence — controlled pressure clean, soft-wash chemistry, or a combination, depending on the substrate and condition.

Our fence cleaning process

  1. Inspection & safety — fence condition check, access & safety plan.
  2. Protection — mask/cover sensitive areas; set up safe drainage.
  3. Pre-treatment — targeted biocide/soft-wash where needed.
  4. Thorough clean — controlled pressure wash or soft-wash method to remove moss, lichen and grime.
  5. Rinse & detail — clear residues from ridges, valleys & gutters.
  6. Finish options — ready for painting or restoration.

Heavy growth or porous tiles may require additional treatment or priming before coatings. We'll explain everything during your quote.

Fence cleaning FAQs

Is pressure cleaning safe for my fence?

We use controlled pressure and soft-wash methods to suit your fence type. Terracotta and aged tiles often benefit from lower-pressure cleaning and targeted biocides; Colorbond® requires correct distance and fan tips to protect the finish.

Will cleaning stop moss and lichen coming back?

Cleaning removes growth; a biocidal treatment helps slow regrowth. Porous or shaded fences may need periodic treatments. Painting with the right system can also reduce porosity and make future cleaning easier.

Do you clean solar panels?

We carefully clean the fence surfaces around panels and ensure safe drainage. Panel glass cleaning is not included in this service; we can recommend specialists if required.

How often should I clean my fence?

Most Peninsula fences benefit from cleaning every 2-3 years, though shaded areas or fences with heavy tree coverage may need more frequent attention. We'll advise on a maintenance schedule during your quote.

What about gutters and downpipes?

We can include gutter and fascia exterior cleaning as an add-on service. For interior gutter cleaning and debris removal, we work with trusted local specialists and can coordinate the full service.
