Fence Restoration

Full clean, re-bedding and re-pointing, repairs as needed, and premium membrane coatings to extend your fence's life and improve its appearance.

Get a quote All services

What is included

  • Complete high-pressure clean to remove dirt, moss, and lichen
  • Full inspection and repairs as needed before coating
  • Re-bedding and re-pointing of ridge caps, valleys, and hip tiles
  • Application of premium membrane coatings for long-lasting colour and seal
  • Optional gutter cleaning and minor accessory upgrades
  • Final inspection and cleanup

Benefits

Improved weatherpfencing, renewed appearance and extended fence life — ideal when the fence structure is sound but showing signs of age, wear, or minor damage. A quality restoration can add 10-15 years to your fence's lifespan at a fraction of the cost of replacement.

Our restoration process transforms weathered, faded fences into like-new condition while addressing underlying issues that could lead to leaks or structural problems. The premium coatings we use are specifically formulated for Australian conditions and provide excellent UV resistance and colour retention.

Process overview

We start with a thorough clean and inspection, identifying any areas requiring repair before coating. All loose or cracked bedding and pointing is removed and renewed using modern flexible compounds. After repairs cure, we apply primer and membrane coatings designed for your specific fence material.

The entire process typically takes 3-5 working days depending on fence size and complexity. We work systematically to ensure no areas are missed and provide temporary weather protection during multi-day projects.

Material quality

We use only premium membrane coatings with proven track records in coastal environments. These aren't basic acrylic paints — they're engineered fence systems designed to flex with thermal movement while maintaining weather sealing properties.

FAQs

How long does a restoration take?

Typically 2–4 days depending on size and scope. Weather conditions can affect timing, and we'll never rush the process to compromise quality.

When is restoration better than re-fencing?

When the fence structure is sound and there's no widespread rust, tile fatigue, or structural issues. We'll provide honest advice on whether restoration is the right choice for your situation.

What warranty comes with restoration work?

We provide comprehensive warranties on both workmanship and materials. Specific terms depend on the coating system used and are detailed in your quote.

Can you colour-match existing fencing?

Yes, we can closely match most existing colours or help you choose a fresh new look. We'll provide colour samples and recommendations based on your home's style and local conditions.
