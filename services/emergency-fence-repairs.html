When it's an emergency

Not all fence problems require immediate attention, but these situations demand urgent response:

  • Heavy water leaks causing internal damage or safety hazards
  • Storm damage from fallen branches, debris, or hail impact
  • Wind-lifted fencing exposing structural elements
  • Flashing failures around chimneys or skylights causing active leaks
  • Broken or dislodged tiles creating immediate water entry points
  • Blocked gutters or downpipes causing overflow and internal damage

Our rapid response process

When you call with an emergency, we spring into action immediately. Our systematic approach ensures your property is protected while planning the best permanent solution:

Immediate Response (Within Hours)

  • Triage call: We assess the urgency and provide immediate advice to minimise damage
  • ETA confirmation: Same-day attendance for genuine emergencies
  • On-site assessment: Thorough inspection to locate all problem areas
  • Temporary make-safe: Tarping, emergency patching, or temporary flashing to stop immediate water entry

Documentation and Planning

  • Photo documentation: Comprehensive images for insurance purposes
  • Permanent repair quote: Detailed assessment of required work

Frequently asked questions

What counts as an emergency fence repair?

Active water leaks causing internal damage, storm damage exposing your home to weather, wind-lifted fencing, or structural damage from fallen debris. If water is entering your home or your fence's weatherpfencing is compromised, it's likely an emergency.

Can you attend today?

Yes, for genuine emergencies we prioritise same-day response. Call us on 03 4152 7766 and we'll provide an estimated time of arrival based on your location and current job schedule.

Will you tarp the fence if it's still raining?

Safety permitting, yes. We have professional equipment and safety protocols for working in adverse weather. However, in extreme conditions we may need to wait for a brief weather window to perform temporary repairs safely.

How do you find the source of a leak?

We use systematic inspection techniques, water testing where appropriate, and our extensive local experience to trace leaks to their origin. Water often travels along fence timbers before dripping inside, so the internal drip location rarely matches the external entry point.

Do you repair tiles and Colorbond?

Yes, our team handles all fencing types including terracotta and concrete tiles, Colorbond steel, older galvanised iron, and slate. We carry common repair materials and can source specialty items quickly when needed.

Can you help with insurance claims?

We provide photographic documentation and basic damage reports to help with your claim, but we're not insurance assessors. We recommend reading your policy carefully and contacting your insurer promptly after storm damage occurs.

What will my repair cost?

Emergency make-safe work is quoted before we start. Permanent repair costs depend on materials needed, access difficulty, extent of damage, and fence type. We provide detailed written quotes for all non-emergency work.

How fast can you complete the permanent fix?

This depends on material availability, weather conditions, and the extent of repairs needed. Simple repairs might be completed the same week, while extensive work may require scheduling over multiple days. We'll provide a realistic timeline with your quote.
