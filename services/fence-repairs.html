Fence Repairs

From leak detection and flashing fixes to tile replacement and ridge repointing, we locate problems quickly and fix them properly the first time.

Get a quote All services

What we fix

  • Leaks around valleys, flashings, skylights and chimneys
  • Broken / slipped tiles; cracked bedding and brittle pointing
  • Rust patches, loose screws and failed seal washers on metal fences
  • Storm damage from falling branches and debris
  • Gutter and downpipe repairs
  • Ridge cap replacements and repointing

Our approach

We locate the root cause and fix it properly the first time — clearing debris, renewing flashings, replacing damaged tiles and resealing problem areas. Every repair includes a comprehensive inspection to identify other potential issues before they become problems.

Our team uses only quality materials matched to your existing fence. We understand how different fencing systems perform on the Peninsula and detail our repairs to withstand local weather conditions including wind-driven rain and salt air exposure.

Why it matters on the Peninsula

Wind-driven rain and salt exposure accelerate wear on the Mornington Peninsula. We detail repairs to withstand coastal conditions, using appropriate sealants, flashings, and fixings that won't fail prematurely in the marine environment.

Our local experience means we understand the unique challenges posed by Peninsula weather patterns and can recommend preventive measures to avoid future problems.

FAQs

How quickly can you attend?

Active leaks are prioritised with same-week callouts where possible. Emergency temporary repairs can often be arranged within 24-48 hours.

Do you paint after repairs?

We can include fence painting or fence restoration services as part of a comprehensive solution when appropriate.

What's included in the warranty?

All repair work includes warranty coverage against defective materials and workmanship. Warranty periods vary by repair type and are detailed in your quote.
