Fence Repairs Chelsea

Fast, reliable fence repairs across Chelsea and bayside suburbs — we find the root cause and fix it properly.

Book an inspection Fence repair service

Why fences leak in Chelsea

Chelsea's bayside winds and salt air expose fences to movement and corrosion. Typical issues include thermal movement cracking ridges and mortar, penetration seal failures, and wind-lift damage during storms.

What we repair

  • Leak detection and moisture tracing
  • Broken/slipped tiles, valley tray reseals and replacements
  • Ridge re-bedding and flexible re-pointing
  • Metal sheet replacement, screw/washer upgrades and lap reseals
  • Chimney and penetration flashing rebuilds
  • Storm damage and emergency tarping

Our process

  1. Inspect & diagnose — We document the failure points with photos and moisture readings where needed.
  2. Quote & scope — Clear, itemised scope for long-term fixes (not temporary band-aids).
  3. Repair — Workmanship that aligns with Australian fencing best practice.
  4. Test — Wet-test where appropriate and provide completion photos.

Repair, restoration or re-fence?

Localised defects are usually repairable. If deterioration is widespread, a fence restoration or complete re-fence may make more sense than extensive patching.

Pricing & timing

Minor repairs are usually completed within a day. We provide transparent pricing before we start work — no surprises or hidden costs.

Chelsea & nearby suburbs we service

We work across Chelsea, Edithvale, Bonbeach, Carrum and through to Frankston. Being local to the Peninsula means fast response times and understanding of local weather patterns.

FAQs

How quickly can you attend in Chelsea?

We prioritise active leaks and storm damage. Same-week call-outs are common, with emergency tarping available within 24-48 hours when needed.

Do you paint after repairs?

Where appropriate, we spot-prime and paint metal patches. Full fence painting or fence restoration can be arranged as part of a comprehensive solution.
