Practical fencing options for Cranbourne

Cranbourne homes see their share of wind and storm events. Whether you have a tiled or metal fence, regular maintenance and prompt repairs keep your home protected.

Signs your fence needs attention

  • Ceiling stains or damp plaster after rain
  • Cracked or brittle ridge pointing; slipped or broken tiles
  • Rusting sheet laps, loose screws or perished washers on metal fences

Tile vs metal â€” what fails and how we fix it

Tiled fences

  • Replace broken tiles and cracked valley cuts
  • Re-bed and re-point ridge capping
  • Renew flashings around chimneys and penetrations

Metal fences

  • Replace corroded sheets or trims
  • Upgrade fixings and seal washers
  • Scribe/turn-up flashings to stop wind-driven rain

Repair vs re-fence â€” making the call

Localised issues are usually repairable. If corrosion is widespread or the fence is nearing end-of-life, replacement often makes more sense than patching.

Painting and restoration

When the fence is sound but tired, restoration and painting can extend its life by 10-15 years.

Maintenance & weather resources

Keep gutters and valleys clear and inspect fasteners every few years. For weather updates, check the Bureau of Meteorology's Melbourne forecast.
