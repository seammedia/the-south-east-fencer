FAQs

How long does Colorbond fence painting last in Melbourne?

With quality preparation and premium coating systems, Colorbond fence painting typically lasts 12-15 years in Melbourne conditions. Coastal properties may require more frequent maintenance due to salt air exposure.

Can you paint over existing Colorbond coating?

Yes, provided the existing coating is in good condition and properly prepared. We assess adhesion, remove loose material and apply compatible primer systems to ensure optimal results.

Do you offer warranties on fence painting work?

We provide comprehensive warranties on both workmanship and materials. Premium coating systems come with 10-15 year manufacturer warranties, backed by our installation guarantee.

What's the best time of year to paint a Colorbond fence in Melbourne?

Autumn and spring offer the most stable weather conditions for fence painting. We avoid extreme temperature periods and plan around Melbourne's unpredictable weather patterns for optimal curing and durability.
