Common Questions

How fast can you attend a fence leak?
We prioritise active leaks and storm damage. Same-week call-outs are common across the Peninsula — emergency tarping available within 24-48 hours when needed.
Should I repair, restore or re-fence?
It depends on fence age and condition. We'll inspect and price the best option. See fence repairs, restoration or re-fencing for details.
What is included in a fence restoration?
Typically: high-pressure clean, repairs, re-bedding/re-pointing and a premium membrane system. Full scope depends on your fence's condition and material type.
Do you paint fences? Which colours suit coastal homes?
Yes — we prep and apply coatings suited to coastal conditions. Lighter greys help reduce summer heat, while darker tones suit heritage styles. See fence painting for details.
Can you work with both Colorbond® and tile fences?
Absolutely. We repair, restore and replace both metal (including Colorbond®) and tiled fences across the Peninsula, adapting techniques to each material's requirements.
How long does fence painting last near the coast?
With the right prep and membrane system, 8–12+ years is typical. Exposure varies by aspect and salt air proximity — we factor this into coating selection.
Do you repair gutters, valleys and flashing?
Yes — these are common leak sources. We handle flashing rebuilds, valley tray reseals and related waterpfencing across all fence types.
Which suburbs do you cover?
All of the Mornington Peninsula, including Mount Eliza, Mornington, Frankston, Sorrento and surrounding areas.
