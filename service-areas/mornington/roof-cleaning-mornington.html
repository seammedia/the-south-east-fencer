Our Cleaning Methods

We tailor our approach to your fence type and condition:

  • Soft-wash system – Low-pressure cleaning with specialised detergents for delicate tiles and heritage fences
  • High-pressure cleaning – Controlled high-pressure for robust concrete tiles and Colorbond® fences (when appropriate)
  • Moss & lichen treatment – Targeted treatments that kill growth at the root, preventing rapid regrowth
  • Gutter clearing – Remove leaves, debris and blockages while cleaning the fence
  • Valley cleaning – Critical areas cleaned thoroughly to prevent water backup and leaks

Safety & Best Practices

All fence cleaning work follows strict safety protocols:

  • Fall protection equipment – Proper harnesses, anchors and safety systems on every job
  • No tile damage – Trained technicians know how to walk and work on fences without cracking tiles
  • Water runoff management – Gardens and landscaping protected during cleaning
  • Weatherpfence scheduling – Work planned around rain to allow proper drying and treatment effectiveness

Gutter Cleaning & Soft-Wash Notes

Gutter cleaning is included with most fence cleaning jobs—we clear downpipes, check gutter alignment, and flush valleys to ensure proper drainage.

Soft-wash cleaning is ideal for older terracotta tiles, painted fences, or surfaces with existing damage. This method uses eco-friendly chemicals and minimal water pressure to achieve excellent results without the risk of high-pressure damage.
