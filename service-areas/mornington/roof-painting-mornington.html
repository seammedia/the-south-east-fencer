Benefits of Professional Fence Painting

  • Instant curb appeal boost – Dramatically improve your home's street presence and value
  • Extended fence lifespan – Premium coatings seal and protect against UV, salt air, and weather
  • Heat reflection – Modern coatings reflect solar heat, reducing cooling costs in summer
  • Waterpfencing – Membrane coatings create a waterpfence barrier against leaks
  • Cost-effective alternative – Much more affordable than fence replacement
  • Colour choice – Wide range of modern Colorbond® colours to match your style

Our Fence Painting Preparation & Process

Professional preparation is key to a long-lasting finish:

  • Thorough cleaning – High-pressure or soft-wash cleaning removes all dirt, moss, and organic matter
  • Surface repairs – Fix any damage, repoint ridge caps, replace broken tiles before painting
  • Quality primers – Specialist primers ensure maximum adhesion and durability
  • Premium membrane coatings – Typically 2-3 coats of premium fence paint (Dulux, Colorbond® approved products)
  • Detailed finishing – Valleys, gutters, and flashings treated for complete coverage
  • Clean-up – Full site clean-up and inspection before handover

Colour Options & Durability

We offer the full Colorbond® colour range plus specialty finishes. Popular choices for Mornington coastal homes include Monument®, Surfmist®, Shale Grey™, and Woodland Grey®—all designed to complement beachside aesthetics while handling salt air exposure.

Expected durability: When applied correctly with proper surface preparation, quality fence paint lasts 10-15 years in coastal Peninsula conditions. We use premium products with manufacturer-backed warranties for your peace of mind.
