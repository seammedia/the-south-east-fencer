What Qualifies as a Fence Emergency?

Call us immediately if you're experiencing:

  • Active water leak – Water entering your home, especially near electrics or into occupied rooms
  • Storm damage – Fallen tree branches, wind-lifted tiles, or visible holes in the fence
  • Structural damage – Sagging fence sections, collapsed areas, or visible structural failure
  • Missing or displaced fencing – Tiles blown off, metal sheets lifted, or large sections compromised
  • Fire or weather event damage – Immediate aftermath of fire, hail, or severe storm
  • Safety hazards – Any fence condition that poses immediate danger to occupants

Our Emergency Response Window

Same-day or next-day response for genuine emergencies across Mornington and the Peninsula. We prioritise:

  • Active leaks causing interior damage
  • Storm damage making properties unsafe or unsecured
  • Situations where delay would cause significant additional damage

After-hours availability: Call 03 4152 7766 any time for emergency assistance. We'll assess your situation and arrange urgent attendance when needed.

Temporary Fixes vs Permanent Repairs

Emergency make-safes include:

  • Tarp installation – Heavy-duty tarps secured to protect against rain until permanent repairs possible
  • Temporary patching – Quick fixes to stop active leaks and prevent further water ingress
  • Debris removal – Clear fallen branches or damaged materials creating hazards
  • Board-up services – Secure exposed areas against weather and entry

Permanent repairs follow once the emergency is stabilised. We'll provide a detailed quote for complete restoration including insurance documentation assistance if needed.
